Spaghetti and Meatballs Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 24 ounces marinara sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Grated mozzarella cheese for topping

Instructions:

  1. Combine ground beef, bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, egg, garlic, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Mix well and form into meatballs.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Pour marinara sauce over the meatballs. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. While the meatballs are cooking, boil a pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente.
  5. Serve the cooked spaghetti topped with the meatballs and sauce. Sprinkle with grated mozzarella cheese.
